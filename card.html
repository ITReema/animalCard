Flamingo

Flamingos are one of the most interesting types of animals in the world. They are different from many other types of birds because of the length of their legs.

Scientific Name: Phoenicopterus
Height: 120-145 cm
Life expectancy: 40 years
Habitat: in variety locations.

The coloring for a Flamingo ranges from various shapes of pink all the way to a crimson red. Some of them are shades of orange too. They also have colors of cream and white mixed in. They are lovely creatures and they seem to be very calm.
